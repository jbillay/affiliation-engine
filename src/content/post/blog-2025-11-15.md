## Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 5 vs. Z Flip 5: Which Foldable Phone Wins?

Foldable smartphones are revolutionizing how we use mobile devices, and Samsung leads the pack with its Galaxy Z series. The latest models, the **Galaxy Z Fold 5** and **Galaxy Z Flip 5**, offer unique experiences tailored to different user preferences. Whether you prioritize productivity or portability, this comparison will help you decide which one is right for you.

### **Design & Build Quality**
The **Galaxy Z Flip 5** is compact, folding vertically like a classic flip phone, making it ultra-portable. Its sleek design fits easily in pockets and purses, while the **Z Fold 5** unfolds horizontally into a tablet-like form, ideal for multitasking.

Both phones feature premium materials, including Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 for durability. However, the **Z Flip 5** is lighter (187g vs. 253g), making it a better choice for those who want a lightweight device.

### **Display & Performance**
The **Z Fold 5** boasts a **7.6-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X** main display and a **6.2-inch cover screen**, perfect for work and multimedia. In contrast, the **Z Flip 5** has a **6.7-inch FHD+ main display** and a **1.8-inch Super AMOLED cover screen**, which is more suited for quick checks.

Under the hood, both run on **Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 for Galaxy**, ensuring smooth performance. However, the **Z Fold 5** supports **S Pen Pro**, a game-changer for note-taking and precision tasks.

### **Cameras & Battery Life**
The **Z Fold 5** has a **50MP main + 12MP ultrawide + 10MP telephoto** setup, while the **Z Flip 5** features a **12MP main + 12MP ultrawide**. Both capture great photos, but the **Z Fold 5** edges out with its telephoto lens.

Battery life is a key factor—the **Z Fold 5** packs a **4,400mAh** battery, while the **Z Flip 5** has **3,700mAh**. The **Z Fold 5** lasts longer, but the **Z Flip 5** supports **25W fast charging**, ensuring quick top-ups.

### **Price & Availability**
The **Galaxy Z Flip 5** starts at **$999**, while the **Z Fold 5** is priced at **$1,799**. If you’re looking for a budget-friendly foldable, the **Z Flip 5** is the better choice. However, if you need a powerhouse for work, the **Z Fold 5** is worth the investment.
