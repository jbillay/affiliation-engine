Smart glasses have evolved from simple Bluetooth headsets to sophisticated AI-driven wearables. ### Key Features of AI Smart Glasses

1. **Voice Control & AI Assistants** – Many smart glasses now support **voice-activated AI assistants** like Google Assistant or Alexa, allowing you to send messages, set reminders, and even control smart home devices—all without touching your phone.
2. **Augmented Reality (AR) Displays** – Some models project digital information directly onto your field of vision, making navigation, translations, and real-time data access effortless.
3. **Hands-Free Connectivity** – Whether you're driving, working, or multitasking, smart glasses keep you connected without needing to look at your phone.
4. **Health & Fitness Tracking** – Advanced models track your heart rate, steps, and even sleep patterns, making them a great fitness companion.
