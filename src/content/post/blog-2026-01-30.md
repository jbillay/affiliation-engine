- **Notion AI** ([Try Notion AI](https://notion.so/product)) can generate meeting notes, summarize emails, and even suggest project organization.
- **Cloak AI** ([Try Cloak AI](https://cloakai.com)) detects links from just a few keywords, saving you time while researching.

Staring at a blank page? **AI writing tools** can help you draft emails, reports, and even blog posts in seconds. Here’s how:

- **Jasper AI** ([Try Jasper](https://www.jasper.ai/)) is great for generating marketing content, product descriptions, or social media posts.
- **Grammarly** ([Try Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/)) ensures your writing is polished while also suggesting tone adjustments.
