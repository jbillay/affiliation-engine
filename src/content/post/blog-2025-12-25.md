## What Are AI-Powered Smart Homes?

AI-powered smart homes use AI and machine learning to automate and optimize daily tasks. These homes can learn your habits, adjust settings based on your preferences, and even anticipate your needs. From voice-activated assistants to self-learning thermostats, AI is making homes more responsive and efficient than ever before.
