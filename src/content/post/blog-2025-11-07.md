### **Why Use an External GPU?**
Before diving into the best options, let’s break down why an eGPU is a game-changer:

- **Boost Performance**: Laptops often have weaker GPUs compared to desktops. An external GPU can drastically improve frame rates and visual fidelity.
- **Portability**: While not as compact as a laptop, an eGPU setup is still far more portable than a full gaming desktop.
- **Future-Proofing**: Upgrade your graphics card without needing a new laptop.
