## Top Features to Look For

When shopping for AI-powered smart glasses, here are the key features to consider:

1. **Voice Control**: Hands-free operation is a must. Look for glasses with robust voice assistants like Google Assistant or Alexa.
2. **AR Capabilities**: Augmented reality features can overlay digital information onto the real world, making navigation and learning more interactive.
3. **Camera & Recording**: Some models come with built-in cameras for capturing photos and videos on the go.
4. **Battery Life**: Since these glasses are always connected, battery life is crucial. Aim for models that offer at least 12 hours of use.
5. **Compatibility**: Ensure the glasses work seamlessly with your smartphone and other devices.
